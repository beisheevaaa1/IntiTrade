
Create a complete end-to-end UI/UX design system and full website design for a university-exclusive second-hand marketplace platform called “IntiTrade — Buy & Sell Within INTI”.

IMPORTANT:
This is a FULL WEBSITE DESIGN request, not only a homepage.
Design all major pages and states of the platform in a consistent visual system.
The platform should look original, professional, modern, clean, and trustworthy.
Use the provided visual inspiration only as a structural and usability reference for a marketplace-style interface. Do not copy it exactly.
Create an original interface specifically branded for IntiTrade.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

IntiTrade is a web-based marketplace designed exclusively for INTI International University students, staff members, and professors. The platform allows verified members of the INTI community to buy and sell second-hand items within a safer and more trusted campus environment.

The platform solves common issues found in public marketplaces such as lack of trust, unsafe interactions with strangers, fake users, poor accountability, and the absence of campus-specific features.

Only users with valid INTI institutional email addresses can register and access the system.
